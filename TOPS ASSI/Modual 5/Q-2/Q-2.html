Q-2 How to embed audio and video in a webpage?

[1]Audio:-

In HTML5, you can use the <audio> tag for embedding an audio file into your web page. Here's
how:
<audio controls autoplay loop>
<source src="horse.mp3" type="audio/mpeg" />
</audio>

The "controls" attribute adds play/stop buttons, volume control, etc., while the "autoplay", "loop" attributes make it start playing automatically when the page loads and keep looping until stopped manually.

[2]Video:-

how:
<video width="400" height="300" controls autoplay loop>
<source src="movie.mp4" type="video/mp4" />
</video>

The "controls" attribute adds play/pause/volume controls, while the "loop" attribute makes it keep playing from start when finished.
