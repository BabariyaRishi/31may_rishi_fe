Q-17 What are the disadvantages of using innerHTML in JavaScript?

=>>When We use innerHTML,it directly updates the html structure.
=>>When We use innerHTML our website is not secure.
=>>We use innerHTML, our website performance is slow and poor.
=>>We use innerHTML some browser not fully support innerHTML.
Example:-
<div id="myDiv">
<p>This is some content.</p>
</div>
<script>
document.getElementById('myDiv').innerHTML += '<p>Appended content.</p>';
</script>
